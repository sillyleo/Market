{% filter markdown %} ### C + Collaboration In order to encourage artists to share their ideas and works with people, CAMPOBAG invited them to join C+ Collaboration. In C+ Collaboration, artists benefit from an Asia tour where they could not only show their creations but also experience different cultures and foreign life styles. Also, they received different marketing recourses from individualized promotional activities. Sponsors are satisfied with different regional markets, sharing their ideas with artists at the same time.CAMPOBAG welcomes any innovations or creativity in sharing. ### How would you fix the world? What project would you create to FIX THE WORLD, if you had a pen and a piece of paper ? CAMPOBAG presents an experimental project to inspire introspection in society. This project will motivate people to think about how to make an influence on world by connecting art with life. ### C-Virus installation Through the theme “Fix the World” of CAMPOBAG, we want to repair human’s fear of viruses. The lack of understanding of diseases and the fear of unknown viruses are crucial. People usually overreact when new viruses reveal. For instance, the rabies out breaking in Taiwan last year caused a lot of dogs to be abandoned and poisoned. If we have more knowledge of unknown diseases, we could avoid ignorant behavior. CAMPOBAG hopes that C-Virus exhibition can remind us not to freak out so easily. ### Contact test@example.com {% endfilter %}
